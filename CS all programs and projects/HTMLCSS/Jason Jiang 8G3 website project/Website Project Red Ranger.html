Jayden Shiba is the Red Samurai Ranger, is the current head of the Shiba Clan and the leader of the Samurai Rangers. He pilots the Lion Folding Zord and controls the element of fire. In the first episode of season 2, "Super Samurai" Jayden leads the team into battle while Antonio finishes the Black Box, giving the Rangers new Zord combos and a new mode: Super Samurai Mode. Super Samurai SS Red Zord Symbols LionZord, TigerZord, and Bull Zord symbols Later in the same season, Jayden gains the power to summon the SharkZord and BullZord. He also gets the Bullzooka. Along with the bull themed blaster and zord, the guardian of the Bullzord, The Grand Shogun, grants the Jayden and his original team power to the ancient Shogun Mode.
